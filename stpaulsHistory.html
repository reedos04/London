St Pauls Cathedral

History

St Pauls Cathedral was designed by Christopher Wren and constructed during 1675-1710.
It is the 4th version of the cathedral after the great fire of 1666 destroyed the previous one.
Located at Ludgate Hill (the highest point of City of London) St Pauls stands out from any view in central London with
one of the tallest domes in the world at 366 feet / 111 meters.
